Advantages of Cross-Platform Apps

Developing cross-platform solutions provides wide consumer reach and effective marketing. This is especially important, for instance, when you are creating a complex eCommerce solution or a full-fledged marketplace. Cross-platform applications are the easiest path to building omnichannel solutions where apps share a single codebase and therefore require much less effort from the developing team.

They also allow companies to lower expenses as you don’t have to involve native developers to build separate versions of the app for each platform. This is also the case with time-to-market: cross-platform apps can be delivered in a rather short period of time compared to native app development.

Hybrid apps and PWAs in some cases also offer higher performance than native apps.
