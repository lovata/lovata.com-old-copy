Cloud Computing Deployment Models

  • Private

    Private cloud solutions allow your business to reside on private cloud infrastructure leaving you with the privileges to maintain your own data centers, applying strict security and privacy policies.

  • Public

    We employ the capabilities of public cloud services of your choice, including Microsoft Azure, Amazon, IBM Bluemix, Rackspace OpenStack, to ensure a seamless transition of your services to the cloud.

  • Hybrid

    Hybrid cloud software allows you to scale out features of your private cloud into a public environment, offering you advantages of both models.
