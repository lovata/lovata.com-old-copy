Responsive Website for a UK Language Academy

Customer: WE Bridge Academy
We-bridge.co.uk

WE BRIDGE is a professional language academy located in Cardiff, Wales, United Kingdom. The academy specializes in training of executives, managers and scientists.

LOVATA has designed and developed a website with a new corporate identity and automation of enrollment process, allowing users to apply for courses and make payments. Also, foreign citizens can receive support on issues related to receiving a visa and other relevant topics via private dashboard after signing in.

Technologies:

# October # EcmaScript 2015 (ES6) # PHP # MySQL
