Full-Stack Development Process

We employ Agile development techniques and methodologies to provide our clients with rapid full-stack software and web/mobile development and deliver projects at minimized time to market. Our iterative development process allows us to engage the client at every stage of development, with quality assurance at every step of the process to ensure reliability and high quality of the final solution.

We begin the process with a thorough business and competitive environment analysis. We listen to the client’s requirements and ideas regarding the project, analyse them and apply the constraints of the business’s ecosystem and real world development practices. The result is a technical specification denoting specific elements and components of design & development.

We then move on to the actual development stage, where our team of professional designers and full-stack developers dedicate themselves to bring your project to life via a full-fledged solution. You oversee the whole process of development and are free to make any alterations at any step of the way. The result is a collaborative effort and hopefully a successful project.
