Car Insurance Aggregator: Web App, Mobile Apps and CRM

Customer: GoZebra

GoZebra is the most popular car insurance policies aggregator on the territory of Kazakhstan. The solution provides a variety of services: from comparing car insurance companies, coverage and rates to ordering the most appropriate auto insurance policy.

The project consists of the following components:

  • Web application that is used to attract customers and generate vehicle insurance requests;
  • Mobile apps for iOS and Android that duplicate the website functionality to simplify the process of applying for auto insurance;
